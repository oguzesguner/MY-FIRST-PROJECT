TURKISH RICE

Ingredients

  • 2 bowls of dry rice.
  • 4 bowls of water
  • 100 gr Butter
  • 1 tbsp of salt
  • 50 gr şehriye(optional, ask it to your Turkish market

Preparation

  1. Wash the rice by using a strainer.
  2. Melt the butter in the rice pan.
  3. Add the rice (also add the şehriye if you have it) and stir it for 3 minutes.
  4. Spread your rice evenly in the pan.
  5. Put a wooden spoon to the pan and add your water without disturbing the spread.
  6. Close the lid and never open it until you see no water bubbles.
  7. Put your rice in a bowl and flip it on a plate.
  8. Your rice is ready to serve!
