Garlic Pasta

Ingredients

  • 500gr Pasta
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Half tbsp of salt
  • Tomato paste
  • A pinch of oregano
  • Sunflower oil

Preparetion

  1. Boil a pot of water.
  2. Cut or crush the garlic cloves.
  3. Put the pasta to the pot and wait around 3 minutes.
  4. Get a sauce pan or any ordinary pan and put the garlic with sunfower oil
  5. Cook the garlic around 1 minute.
  6. Add the tomato paste and the salt to the pan.
  7. Add a tbsp of water from the pasta pot.
  8. Stir it on low heat until it starts boiling.
  9. Strain your pasta and add it back to the pot.
  10. Add the sauce to the pot and stir under low heat for 2 minutes.
  11. Your garlic pasta is ready to serve!
